---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview user relentlessly about every aspect of plan until shared understanding.

Walk down each branch of design tree. Resolve dependencies between decisions one-by-one.

For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask questions one at a time.

If question answerable by exploring codebase, explore codebase instead of asking.
